HEALTH, SAFETY, ENVIRONMENT POLICY

The management of EXBORNE MULTI-SERVICES LIMITED has a policy that its business should be conducted in such a way that health and safety of its employees and that of other people within its operational areas are preserved and damage to property/equipment if not completely eliminated, shall be brought to the barest minimum. Further, all necessary documents must be maintained.

Under the framework of this policy, the company will strive to instill in staff of our subcontractors a high degree of personal hygiene and at all time, implement work procedures that are free from health risks. Every member of our staff as a matter of routine is medically examined at least once a year for which a certificate of fitness shall be issued. Our company retains the services of a Medical Consultant for this purpose. First Aid Services will be provided in all work sites, as backups. It is also the policy on environment by reducing environmental degrading elements to the barest minimum. We abide by all clients' safety procedures and have necessary permits in all our operations. Our dedicated safety officers are always on-site to enforce safety in all our operations. Security matters shall be given priority as the situation demands.
